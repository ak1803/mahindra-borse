Uro Gynaecology Treatment

Dr. Mahindra Borse - Urogynecology specialist in Pune

Urogynecology is a sub-specialty of Gynecology, and in some nations, it is also known as Female Pelvic Medicine and Reconstructive Surgery. A urogynecologist maintains clinical difficulties connected with the dysfunction of the pelvic floor and bladder. Pelvic floor diseases affect the bladder, reproductive processes, and bowels.

  • Stress Incontinence surgeries : TVT, TOT, TVT-O
  • Burch colposuspension
  • Pelvic Floor Surgeries
  • Lateral cystocoele repair
